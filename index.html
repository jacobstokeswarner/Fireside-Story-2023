It was a dark and stormy night...

Everyone thought they were safe from the storm inside their cabins at camp Crystal Lake...

The rain beat upon the tents of the teens, and as they huddled close to each other, one thought they heard a noise... "CH CH CH, HAH HAH HAH""...

The teens were startled by an older woman, who claimed to be a caretaker, and was "just checking in" on what the teens were doing.

Then, old Jimmy, that ornery rascal, was so startled by the old woman, that he punched her square in the face.
